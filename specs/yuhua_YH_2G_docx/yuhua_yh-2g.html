yuhua YH-2G

Product Name: Small spray dryer (glass drying chamber)

Product model :YH-2G

Maximum feeding capacity: 2000mL/H

Small spray dryer Product Description:

It is suitable for the production of trace particle powder in universities, research institutes and laboratories of food, pharmaceutical and chemical enterprises. It is widely applicable to all solutions such as emulsion and suspension, and is suitable for the drying of heat-sensitive substances such as biological products, biological pesticides, enzyme preparations, etc. Because the ejected material is only exposed to high temperature and is heated instantaneously when it is sprayed into a mist, these active materials maintain their active components unchanged after drying.

Specifically used in the following fields:

Beverages, fragrances, pigments, milk, egg products, plant and vegetable extracts, heat-sensitive substance, plastic polymers, resins, aromatics, blood products, ceramics, superconductors, biochemical products, dyes, soaps, detergents, food adhesives, bone and tooth powders, etc.

Main features of small spray dryer:

  • Original nozzle, high efficiency.
  • Large color touch screen operation, fully automatic control and manual control combined, with Chinese and English panels and parameter LCD display.
  • Real-time regulation of PID constant temperature control technology, accurate temperature control, heating temperature control accuracy ±1℃.
  • The spray, drying and collection devices are made of transparent, high-quality, high-temperature resistant borosilicate glass, and the entire spray drying experiment process can be visualized.
  • The jet injector is automatically dredged (through the needle) and the frequency is adjustable. When the nozzle is blocked, it will automatically clear to ensure the continuity of the experiment.
  • The feed amount can be adjusted by the feed peristaltic pump.
  • Special protection function, the fan does not start, the heater can not start.
  • Built-in imported oil-free air compressor, noise is less than 50db.
  • After drying, the product has uniform particle size.

Small spray dryer configuration:

1, air compressor 2, fan 3, peristaltic pump 4, atomizing nozzle 5, drying room 6, cyclone separator 7,Collection bottle 8, waste liquid bottle 9, heating tube 10, circuit board 11, touch screen 12, 1 set of clamps 13, power switch 14, feed pipe 15, trachea 16, pressure gauge 17, moving wheel 18. exhaust duct
