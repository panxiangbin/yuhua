Heater and Chiller Circulation Device

Model No.GDSZ-10/80+200

Work Principle

Heater and Chiller Circulation Device is also called high and low temperature circulating machine, which is composed of famous brand’s compressor , large exchange plate, magnetic circulation pump and digital control system. The thermal conductivity medium of the whole circulation system is fully closed circulation, circulation system with liquid expansion container, expansion container and liquid circulation is an independent system, expansion container does not participate in liquid circulation, but mechanical connection. Even in the case of high temperature operation, the thermal conductivity medium in the expansion vessel will not be higher than 50 degrees. The whole liquid circulation is a closed system, no water vapor absorption at low temperature, no oil mist at high temperature, improve the service life of thermal oil; The same equipment, the same heat conduction medium can achieve continuous temperature control from -95 degrees to 350 degrees. Cooling capacity 1KW~ 100KW optional.
